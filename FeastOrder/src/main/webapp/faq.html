Ordering

Browse the menu page, choose a category (appetizers, main course, or desserts), and click on any item to see its ingredients, nutritional information, and price. Select your quantity and any add-ons — like extra cheese or a drink — then add it to your cart. When you're ready, go to your cart to review your order and proceed to checkout.
Yes. Browsing the menu is open to everyone, but you'll need to create a free account before checking out, so we can keep track of your order history and send you confirmation details.
02

Account

Click Register and fill in your username, full name, email address, phone number, and a password (minimum 8 characters). Once you submit the form, you'll be redirected to the login page to sign in with your new account.
Yes. Passwords are never stored in plain text, and your account details are only used to process your orders and improve your experience on FeastOrder. We do not share your personal information with third parties without your consent.
03

Payment

FeastOrder currently supports major credit and debit cards (Visa, MasterCard) and cash on delivery. You can choose your preferred method during checkout, before confirming your order.
Yes. After checkout, you'll see an order confirmation page summarizing your items, add-ons, total price, and order time. This is also saved to your account's order history.
If an item is missing, incorrect, or below quality expectations, contact us within 24 hours via the Contact page with your order details, and our team will review it for a refund or replacement.
04

Delivery

Typical delivery time is 30–45 minutes from order confirmation, depending on your location and order volume at the time.
Order status updates (received, preparing, out for delivery, delivered) are shown on your order confirmation and order history pages after logging in.
