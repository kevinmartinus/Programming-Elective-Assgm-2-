Who We Are

Good Food, Made Easy to Order

FeastOrder began as a small neighbourhood kitchen with one simple goal: cook honest, comforting meals and make them easy to order, any time. What started as a handful of regulars ordering by phone has grown into a full online ordering platform — browse the menu, customize your order, and check out in just a few clicks. The recipes, and the care behind them, haven't changed.

Every dish on our menu is prepared fresh using ingredients sourced from local suppliers wherever possible. Whether you're ordering appetizers for a small gathering or a full family dinner, our kitchen treats every order — big or small — with the same attention to detail.
